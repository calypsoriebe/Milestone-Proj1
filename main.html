Tomo-Mon Adventures

Rules and Tips

In this game you play as Dan, Ramona, or Lana. Your goal is to look for your pet Bean! You woke up and have no clue where he went. As you walk around your town you will be able to interact with your environment, and find clues to lead you to bean. The only rules are to have fun, and the only tip is to make sure you read the prompts when interacting with your environment!

Controls

WASD to move around, and various buttons that you will be prompted to press when interacting with your environment.
